BRAND PERFORMANCE REPORT  ·  May 2026
Executive Summary
Organic Performance Offsets Slowdown as Inventory Mix Evolves
May performance demonstrated improving sales stability despite continued inventory constraints across portions of the catalog. Amazon unit sales increased modestly month over month, driven primarily by organic demand, while website performance rebounded with meaningful gains in both units sold and orders.

While advertising activity moderated during the month, organic sales momentum strengthened, particularly on Amazon where organic units increased 8% month over month.
Reverse Shampoo (Travel Size) emerged as the top-selling SKU for Amazon, with Promote and Reverse Activators continuing to rank among the brand's strongest performers. On the website, order volume and unit sales recovered while activators remained the primary driver of demand, highlighting continued consumer loyalty for available SKUs.

As inventory availability improves, the current sales mix provides a strong foundation for future growth.
